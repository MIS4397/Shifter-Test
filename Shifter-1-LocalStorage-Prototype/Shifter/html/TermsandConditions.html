Day Trade Terms and Conditions

Choosing the "Accept Terms" option below holds you bound to the Day Trade Terms and Conditions as specified below as well as to the Fly To Win Day and Shift Trade Guidelines for all day trade actions performed in this session. Choosing the "Do Not Accept Terms" option below returns you to the eTA Main Menu.

Trade Limits

It is the original shift holder's responsibility to:
  1. Limit the amount of trades posted for a single shift.
  2. Ensure the shift is traded off only once.
  3. Remove any multiple postings and overlapping trades once a shift has been accepted.

Day Trade Terms of Use

  1. The qualified team member who signs a shift or day trade form stating that he or she agrees to work the indicated shift becomes responsible to work the shift or day trade hours identified. Station leadership is not required.
  2. An agreed upon shift or day trade can only be canceled after signatures from all involved parties are obtained.
  3. A team member accepting the shift or day trade will clock/swipe in or date his/her time card and receive his/her hourly straight time wage for the trade hours worked, except for company-recognized holidays as referenced in Paragraph B-10.
  4. A team member on a Day Trade Off (DTO) cannot work voluntary overtime during their regular scheduled hours.
  5. Verbal trades are not permitted.

Inappropriate Activities That May Lead to Corrective Actions

  1. The team member accepting the trade fails to show up or call prior to the shift.
  2. Unacceptable level of attendance.
  3. Submitting or accepting a trade that is not valid or in compliance with Fly to Win guidelines.

Corrective Actions

1st Occurrence - Team member will have a documented verbal counseling.
2nd Occurrence - Team member will receive a written warning letter and a 30 day suspension of Shift/Day trade privileges.
3rd Occurrence - Team member will receive a termination warning letter and a 60 day suspension of Shift/Day trade privileges.
4th Occurrence - Team member will be terminated.
